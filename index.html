How We Work

Our Business Model

Khavho Groups generates revenue through its subsidiary companies while serving as the central procurement hub for materials and products.

Central Procurement Hub

Our subsidiary companies procure all their materials and products through Khavho Groups, ensuring quality control and cost efficiency.

Open to External Clients

Outside companies and individuals can also purchase materials and products directly from us, expanding our market reach.

Integrated Financial Solutions

Through Khavho Capital & Investment, we provide financing options to help clients acquire the products they need.

About Khavho Group

Building excellence through innovation, integrity, and unwavering commitment to our clients and communities.

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Khavho Group has grown from a small construction company into one of South Africa's most respected business conglomerates. Our journey began with a simple vision: to build excellence in everything we do.

Today, we operate through three specialized subsidiaries, each a leader in its respective field. Our integrated approach allows us to offer comprehensive solutions that span the entire business lifecycle, from construction and development to investment and financial services.

With over 7 years of experience, we have successfully completed more than 500 projects and maintained a 98% client satisfaction rate. Our success is built on the foundation of our core values and the dedication of our exceptional team.

The principles that guide every decision we make and every project we undertake.
