About Khavho Groups

Building Excellence Across Industries

Our Story

Khavho Groups is a leading diversified business conglomerate with over a decade of excellence in driving economic growth across Africa. Established in 2012, we have evolved from a single construction company into a multi-faceted organization with three core divisions: Khavho Capital, Khavho Holdings, and Khavho Inter-Africa.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to build not just structures, but sustainable businesses that create lasting value for our communities. Today, we are proud to be recognized as one of Africa's most trusted business partners, with a portfolio spanning construction, investment management, financial services, and international trade.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional services across multiple industries while maintaining the highest standards of integrity, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We are committed to building lasting partnerships that drive sustainable growth and create positive impact across Africa.

Our Vision

To be Africa's premier business conglomerate, recognized for excellence, innovation, and sustainable growth across all our business sectors. We envision a future where African businesses thrive through strategic partnerships, innovative solutions, and unwavering commitment to quality.
