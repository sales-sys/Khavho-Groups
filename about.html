About Khavho Groups

Building Excellence Across South Africa

Our Story

Khavho Groups is a diversified business conglomerate specializing in construction, investment management, and financial services. Since our establishment, we have been committed to driving economic growth across South Africa through innovation, integrity, and excellence.

Our journey began with a vision to build sustainable businesses that create lasting value for our communities. Today, we operate through three core divisions: Khavho Capital, Khavho Holdings, and Khavho Inter-Africa, each bringing specialized expertise to serve our clients' diverse needs.
